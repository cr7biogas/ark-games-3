📋 REGOLE GIUDICE

Movement Standards

  • Squat: Hip crease below knee at bottom, full extension at top
  • Push-up: Chest to ground, full lockout at top
  • Pull-up: Chin over bar, full arm extension at bottom
  • Deadlift: Full lockout hips and knees, controlled descent
  • Clean: Bar starts from ground, caught in front rack, full stand

No-Rep Criteria

  • Range of motion incomplete
  • Movement not controlled
  • Standard not met at start or finish position

Scoring

  • Count only valid reps
  • For Time: record completion time
  • AMRAP: record total reps
  • Submit score immediately after athlete completes
